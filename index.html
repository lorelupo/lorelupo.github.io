Lorenzo Lupo

I am concluding my PhD in machine learning and natural language processing at Université Grenoble-Alpes, team GETALP. In the last three years, I focused on context-aware neural machine translation, supervised and supported by Laurent Besacier and Marco Dinarelli. Previously, I have worked on automatic grammatical error correction at Indigo AI and on reinforcement learning with Marcello Restelli at Politecnico di Milano. Besides research, I mostly study and practice meditation, read, spend time with my family and friends, go to the gym and maintain a blog on easy vegan cuisine with my partner and my syster.
